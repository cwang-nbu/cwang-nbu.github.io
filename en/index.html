Chong Wang

Associate Professor

Ningbo University

Biography

Chong Wang received his B. Eng degree from Zhejiang University of Technology in 2007, M. Eng degree from University of Science and Technology of China in 2010, and his Ph.D. degree from The University of Hong Kong in 2014. He was a research assistant and research associate in The University of Hong Kong and Hong Kong Polytechnic University, respectively. Since 2015, he has been with the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, Ningbo University, where he is currently an associate professor.

His main research interests are in gesture recognition, human computer interaction, pattern recognition, image and video restoration and image based rendering
